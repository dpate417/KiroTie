📋 CSV Format Specification

Your CSV or Excel file must include these columns. Optional columns can be left blank.

Column Required Type Accepted Values Example
event_name Optional Text Any string Spring Club Fair
event_type ✅ Required Text food_social · academic_workshop · career_fair · club_meeting · general food_social
expected_signups ✅ Required Integer Any number ≥ 1 120
planned_quantity ✅ Required Integer Any number ≥ 1 120
cost_per_person Optional Number Dollar amount (default: 15) 18
time_slot Optional Text prime_time · monday_morning · friday_afternoon · finals_week · midterm_week · weekend friday_afternoon
registration_timing Optional Text same_day · one_day_before · two_to_seven_days · over_one_week · over_two_weeks over_two_weeks
interest_match_score Optional Decimal 0.0 (low) to 1.0 (high) 0.8
historical_show_rate Optional Decimal 0.0 to 1.0 (your past show rate) 0.65

🔧 Bring Your Own Data

Have your own event history? Here's how to convert it to EventWise format.

1 Export your event data from Eventbrite, Google Forms, or your registration system as CSV
2 Rename your columns to match the format above (event_type, expected_signups, planned_quantity)
3 Map your event categories to: food_social · academic_workshop · career_fair · club_meeting · general
4 Upload to EventWise — the Transparency Gate will flag any rows with missing required fields
